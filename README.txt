== Description ==

Connect Posteria to WordPress, enter your favorite topics, watch content flood, share and enrich it automatically, share it to your social media and even your newsletter ... And much more stay tuned ;-)

[youtube https://youtu.be/Wz_98db-TBI]

= Features =

* Enables OAuth and Api on your wordpress and connect it to Posteria
* Receive fresh new content every day about your favorite topics 
* Share it to your blog
* Share it then to your social media to deliver traffic
* Share it to your newsletter for nurturing puroposes and get more customer retension
* Sit down, look at your stats grow and relax :-)

== Installation ==

1. Upload `Posteria` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or use the built in plugin install by WordPress
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. You're Ready to Rock
